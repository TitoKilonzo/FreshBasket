Our Story

Born from a Passion for Fresh Food

FreshBasket started when our founder noticed how hard it was for Nairobi families to access truly fresh, affordable organic produce without visiting crowded markets. We set out to solve that — by going directly to the farmers.

We visit our partner farms weekly to inspect quality and freshness before each delivery batch.
Our cold chain ensures produce arrives at your door as fresh as the day it was harvested.
We are committed to sustainable, eco-friendly packaging across all our deliveries.
Every order directly supports Kenyan smallholder farmers and their families.
